Accessing the Portal

1. Go to the portal website

Use the link provided in your welcome email or on the university website.

2. Enter your username and password

These are the same credentials you use for your university email and other online services.

3. Click "Log In"

You'll be directed to your personalized student portal.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Forgotten Password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the prompts to reset your password.

Incorrect Username?

Double-check that you're using the correct username, including capitalization.
